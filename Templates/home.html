Medicine Supply Chain Management Portal

Medicine Supply Chain Tracker is a blockchain-inspired system that ensures transparency in pharmaceutical distribution. Track every batch from manufacturer to pharmacy with verified records.

Manufacturer Initiates the supply chain. Each batch is recorded as a genesis entry, ensuring authenticity, traceability, and a tamper-proof starting point for the entire distribution process
DistributorManages the secure transfer of medicine batches between entities ensuring continuity in the hash-linked record while maintaining transparency and accountability during the movement
PharmacyActs as the final verification point in the supply chain. They validate quantity and expiry details, and ensure medicines are safe, completing the traceable and verifiable lifecycle of each batch.

Features :

  • Role-based authentication for manufacturers, distributors, pharmacies, and admins.
  • Manufacturer batch creation with medicine details, certificates, and lab reports.
  • Hash-chained records to simulate tamper-proof blockchain transactions.
  • Distributor shipment tracking with transport and delivery information.
  • Pharmacy batch verification for quantity, expiry, and condition.
  • Public QR code scanning to verify batch authenticity and full history.
  • Real-time dashboards showing current batch status and ownership.
  • Automatic tamper alerts if the hash chain integrity is compromised.
  • Search and filter batches by ID, medicine name, or status.
  • Secure document storage and retrieval for certificates and evidence files.
